Perú 2011

Inca rope bridge in Perú is near the town of Quehue, in the Quehue District of the Canas Province of the Cuzco Region.
Name in Spanish is "Puente Colgante Q'eswachaka" (sometimes spelled in both languages as "Keshwa Chaca," among other variations.
