  • Entry/Exit Requirements
    • Czech Republic – passport valid for 3 months beyond extended stay; visa not required
    • Germany – passport; visa not required
    • Greece – passport; visa not required
    • Israel – passport valid for 4 months beyond intended stay, onward or return ticket, proof of sufficient funds; a no-charge 3-month visa may be issued upon arrival
    • West Bank – passport with Israeli stamp at Israeli point of entry
    • Jordan – passport and visa; visa can be issued for a fee at Sheikh Hussein and Arava crossings, but not at King Hussein/Allenby crossing; according to the Allenby Border Terminal website, "Passengers departing for Jordan via the Allenby Bridge Terminal require an entry permit into Jordan, which specifically states that the transit is via the Allenby Bridge Terminal."  Embassy website is http://www.jordanembassyus.org/new/consular/visas.shtml.
    • Egypt – passport and visa. Visa can be purchased at Cairo International Airport for $15, payable in U.S. dollars.
