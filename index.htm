Hello, World!

After several significant trips, I've put together trip reports to highlight descriptions and pictures of the things I've seen. In general, I've also tried to use the trip report as an occasion to play with a new technology that I want to become more proficient at – hence the name of this section: “Hello, World!”

Note, however, that these pages get very little love and attention after I finish them. So, in almost all cases, they reflect a structure that is a product of their time and my point on the learning curve (prominent – but not alone – among these “sins” being the use of <table> for layout).
